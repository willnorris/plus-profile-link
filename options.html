Google+ Profile Link Options

This feature is highly experimental and not recommended for most users. This will call the SGAPI to lookup every page you visit (unless a Google+ profile was already found on the page by other means). This may have both performance and privacy implications. Also be aware that the SGAPI has been deprecated and will be shutdown on April 20, 2012. This feature will likely be removed at that point if an alternative service cannot be found.
